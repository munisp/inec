Campaign Planning Innovation 6 & 7:
Real-Time Performance Intelligence

Debate performance scoring and volunteer network analysis give campaign managers live feedback during the most critical moments.

6. Debate Performance Tracker

Analyzes each statement in real time, scoring sentiment, tone, and thematic coverage.

Grading the overall performance (A–D), the system provides immediate tactical recommendations to the candidate's earpiece or podium screen (e.g., "Avoid defensive language — pivot to solutions").

7. Volunteer Network Graph

Models the social reach of the campaign's volunteer base using advanced graph theory.

Identifies "super-spreader" influencers whose connections can amplify messaging to thousands of additional voters through secondary and tertiary networks.
