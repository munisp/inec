Campaign Planning Innovation 1:
AI Speech Writer Speaks to Every Nigerian

An LLM-backed speech engine generates culturally resonant campaign materials in multiple languages in seconds.

The AI Speech Writer dynamically constructs prompts using candidate metadata, including name, office, state, and key policy priorities.

It generates content directly in the target language rather than translating after the fact, preserving political idioms and cultural resonance specific to each zone.

A deterministic template fallback guarantees zero downtime, ensuring candidates always have access to core messaging during API outages.

Supported Languages

  • English (National)
  • Hausa (North-West / North-East)
  • Yoruba (South-West)
  • Igbo (South-East)

7 Dynamic Speech Types

  • Rally & Manifesto
  • Press Release & Policy Brief
  • Debate Opening
  • Victory & Concession
