Campaign Planning Innovation 4 & 5:
Optimising Money and Movement

A 4-tier fundraising model and a TSP route optimizer ensure efficient deployment of resources.

4. Fundraising Optimizer

Segments donors to calculate expected yield and generates phased event timelines to close funding gaps.

  • Major Donors
  • Mid-tier Contributors
  • Grassroots Supporters
  • Diaspora Network

5. Canvassing Route Optimizer

Implements a nearest-neighbor Traveling Salesperson Problem (TSP) algorithm for geographically efficient door-to-door canvassing routes.

  • Minimizes travel distance across target wards
  • Maximizes daily voter contact
  • Reduces logistical overhead and fuel costs
