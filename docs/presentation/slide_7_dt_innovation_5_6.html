Digital Twin Innovation 5 & 6: AI and Live Data Close the Prediction Gap

A natural-language "What-If" generator and live-data calibration make the simulation engine self-improving in real time.

Innovation 5: AI "What-If" Generator

Administrators can input hypothetical events in plain English (e.g., "What if a major bridge collapses in Kogi State?"). The engine parses the input and maps it to the appropriate simulation parameters automatically.

Innovation 6: Real-Time Calibration

The engine continuously ingests live turnout data from the Fluvio stream on Election Day, recalibrating its predictive models on the fly to reflect ground reality and close the gap between prediction and actuals.
