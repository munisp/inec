Digital Twin Innovation 2: Four-Scenario Parallel Branching

Simultaneous simulation of Baseline, Optimistic, Pessimistic, and Crisis scenarios enables contingency planning.

Baseline
Expected turnout rates and standard logistics performance based on historical averages.
Optimistic
High voter turnout, zero logistical delays, and seamless BVAS operation.
Pessimistic
Lower turnout, minor supply chain disruptions, and isolated technical issues.
Crisis
Severe weather, coordinated BVAS failures, and security incidents occurring simultaneously.

The engine runs all four scenarios in parallel for every simulation request. By incorporating simultaneous adverse events into the Crisis scenario, INEC can pre-position emergency response resources before Election Day. This approach mirrors the rigorous "scenario planning" methodology used by central banks and military planners.
