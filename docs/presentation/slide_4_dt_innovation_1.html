Digital Twin Innovation 1: Monte Carlo Forecasting

A 1,000-run Monte Carlo engine converts uncertain turnout estimates into rigorous probability distributions.

Provides a full probability distribution instead of a single point estimate, giving decision-makers a complete risk picture.

  • 5th Percentile: Worst-case scenario planning (e.g., severe logistics failure)
  • Median (50th): Most likely outcome based on current trajectory
  • 95th Percentile: Best-case scenario planning (e.g., optimal turnout)
