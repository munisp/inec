Digital Twin Innovation 3 & 4: Disruption Modeling

Weather and adversarial attack simulations translate real-world threats into precise turnout suppression percentages.

Innovation 3: Weather & Logistics

Incorporates probabilistic weather events calibrated to Nigeria's six geopolitical zones.

  • Flooding in the South-South
  • Harmattan dust storms in the North
  • Road infrastructure failures in the North-Central

Innovation 4: Adversarial Attacks

Simulates targeted security threats to calculate the resulting percentage of voided or delayed votes per LGA.

  • Ballot box snatching
  • BVAS device failures
  • Polling agent intimidation
